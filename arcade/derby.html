⚾ Home Run Derby

Behind-the-plate prototype

Pitch 0/60 pts

Tap Start, then tap the ball as it crosses the zone.

How to play: The pitcher throws and the ball grows as it comes at you. Tap once to swing. Where you tap is where your bat meets the ball, when you tap is your timing. Square it up in the middle of the zone with good timing for a home run. Catch the top of the ball and you top it (grounder); get under it and you pop it up.
